Tomato Spacing

Plant them about 3 inches deep and 12 inches apart within the rows, leaving about 3 feet of space between the rows.
Watering

Mature and bearing garden tomato plants require 1-2 inches of water per week
Temperature of Soil

Tomatoes grow best at 65-85 degrees.
Weeding

Use mulch 3-5 weeks after germinating the tomato to deter weeds. As the plant matures and grows, the space around the plant becomes to shady and populated by roots to support any weed growth.

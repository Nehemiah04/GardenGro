Celery Spacing

Plant them about 3 inches deep and 12 inches apart within the rows, leaving about 3 feet of space between the rows.
Watering

If you need to water, be sure you do a thorough job, applying enough to moisten the soil 8- to 10-inches below ground. Deep watering is the only way to go.
Temperature of Soil

Germination temperature: 40 F - Do not plant seed potatoes until soil reaches 40 F.
Weeding

Once hills are made, do all weeding by hand, as to not disturb the vegetable.

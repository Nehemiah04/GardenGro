Celery Spacing

Celery seeds should be ¼ to ½ inch deep in Soil, 6 to 10 inches apart; space rows 24 inches apart.
Watering

Keep soil consistently moist by applying about 1 inch of water per week through watering or rainfall.
Temperature of Soil

Plant your Celery seeds outdoors when the soil is moist, well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.
Weeding

Before planting your Celery, weeding or tilling in the weeds several times is ideal.
